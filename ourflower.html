Our Flower


The flower we carry at Chronic Pain Releaf Center is potent, tasty, and comes in many colors, textures, and varieties.


All of our flower is 85-100% organic, and all of it is clean grown. This means that all of the nutrients used to grow and cultivate the flower are natural and certified “safe” ingredients by either the Food and Drug Administration, the Marijuana Enforcement Division, or both.


Marijuana is always classified into two universal types: sativa and indica. Both have different recreational and medical benefits that appeal to a wide range of preferences.


A third type known as: Hybrid is a cross between sativa and indica strains that vary in percentages from sativa dominant to indica dominant.

Strains

Sativa

Sativa strains are what we colloquially refer to as “daytime strains.” This is because sativas are typically characterized as euphoric, focused and energizing, while stimulating creativity in many. Due to their often higher THC content, sativa strains are great for providing an intense happy high, as well as regulating nausea, vomiting, pain and depression. We carry many popular and unusual sativa strains, which we often rotate in and out of our store. Sativa strains are rich in terpenes such as limonene and pinene, which contribute to their often citrusy or piney scents and the overall health benefits of the flower.

Indica

Indica strains create the opposite effect. Often chosen for its sedative qualities, indica strains are most effective at treating things such as anxiety, pain, and insomnia. The high is relaxed and lazy, which is why it’s often called a “body buzz” or an “in-di-couch” effect. Indica strains are often preferred for treating neurological and physical ailments, due to its often higher CBD content. Common indica strains include those from the Kush and OG family, both of which we keep on hand in the shop.

Hybrid

Hybrid strains result when indica and sativa strains are crossed together, which typically result in a comfortable and relaxed high, without being too sedative. The majority of strains that we carry are hybrids to some degree, whether it leans more on the indica side (indica dominant) or sativa side (sativa dominant). This is so that we can reap the euphoric benefits of sativa strains while maintaining the relaxed body buzz from indica strains. Hybrid strains vary immensely in potency and effect and include many popular strains that we carry, such as Blue Dream and Girl Scout Cookies. These hybrid strains characteristically offer a good "all purpose" high.
